# CAIT Bridal Brand Guide

## Brand

- Name: CAIT Bridal
- Tagline: Your affordable wedding boutique
- Direction: Regency-era romance, floral elegance, restrained luxury
- Avoid copying Netflix/Bridgerton logos or exact branded assets. Use the mood only.

## Palette

- Ivory background: `#FBF6EE`
- Soft lavender: `#BCA2C8`
- Deep lavender: `#8E6FA0`
- Blush pink: `#E8C9D1`
- Sage green: `#98A486`
- Antique gold: `#C9A45C`
- Charcoal text: `#332C2F`
- Warm border: `#E5D8CB`

## Typography

- Display serif: elegant high-contrast serif
- Script accent: only for small decorative brand moments, never for body text
- Body: clean readable sans-serif

## Visual rules

- Floral accents should be sparse and decorative, not repeated behind every component.
- Gold is an accent, not a full-page background.
- Buttons should remain readable and modern.
- Keep strong contrast and accessible focus states.
- Use the supplied raster logo temporarily.
- Do not crop the logo tightly around the flowers.
- Later replace it with a transparent SVG or PNG version for production.
